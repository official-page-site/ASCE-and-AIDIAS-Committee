Subject Description

Sotware Engineering deals with reliability and quality assurance of the soltware under development. It provide framework, for development of quality software product. The course enables the students to write specifications for software system, design and develop test plans according to design specification and the process to deploy software. The course also covers important aspects of Software Engineering.
